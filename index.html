CitiBIM

"Delivery world-class technology implements in the Nigerian construction industry."

The CitiBIM group is comprised of passion-driven engineers, project managers, business analysts and researchers who work closely to provide the required platform for delivering advanced design and construction approaches in Nigeria. We aim to provide leadership for young and experienced professionals eager in creating cutting edge world-class architectural designs and structures through trainings and offering expert advice.

We are a team of multi-talented individuals with vast expertise in construction business modelling and analytics, construction informatics, building information modelling, modular construction, infrastructure design and project management, advanced research in construction technologies and world-class training in construction. We are reaching out to all relevant stakeholders in the field of construction and beyond because we believe in innovation, adaptation and imagination to drive this new era in the construction industry in Nigeria.

Our vision

Our Vision is to drive a new era of development, growth and productivity in construction, and improve the way projects are planned, procured, and delivered in Nigeria.

our mission

Our mission is to facilitate the delivery of world-class collaboration of construction stakeholders to implement cutting-edge approaches and technologies in Nigeria.
